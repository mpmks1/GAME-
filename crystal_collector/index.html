Welcome to Crystal Collector!

Game instuctions:
You will be given a random goal number at the beginning of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the goal number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the goal number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.

Press the start button below to begin your game!
